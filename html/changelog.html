04 December 2020

AustationBot updated:

  • The white changeling icons are now fixed and returned to their codersprite glory.
  • fixes a back sprite icon that was not properly mirrored
  • minecart beeing animated
  • refactored some code regarding the crate animation to use actual sphere coordinates instead

  • syringe guns no longer instantly inject their reagents, instead injecting them over time
  • syringes can be embedded on harm intent, also injecting reagents over time, but at a slower rate
  • explosive chem mixes no longer purge chems if an explosion is not triggered
  • A bunch of mob-related code has been improved.
  • For performance reasons, animals will only attempt to mate at a maximum rate of once every 5 minutes.

  • For performance reasons, Kalo's cleaning range has been halved to 5 turfs. I've also lowered the rate at which he finds things to clean.
  • For performance reasons, Cak will only frost a donut when entering a turf with a donut.
  • Several other performance tweaks.
  • Admins now get a message if the AI or a borg decides to delete Crew mainfest entries
  • Doors are smoother
